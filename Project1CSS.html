CSS

CSS is used primarily to beautify your websites through the manipulation of colors, layouts, and fonts. The color control can be specific to one certain element or to every element of a certain type. Also, the changes you can make to color extend from the font itself, to the background and border lines. Like the control over layout displayed in HTML, CSS can move paragraphs and images through your website. Finally the control over font includes font size, alignment, color, and font type. Overall, CSS is used to let you personalize your website and let the presentation of your website be professional.
